Welcome!

Zen Simple Assise is a sangha network of former disciples of Master Deshimaru and other more recent practitioners.

They combine their energy and experience to Dissemination of the practice and teaching transmitted in the Of Master Deshimaru, Master Kodo Sawaki and their predecessors of Zen Soto.

Zen Simple Assise is a common space for meetings, exchanges and Reflection to protect zazen from the current trends and institutions.

We organize different events such as the anniversary of the death of Master Deshimaru a Mokudo sesshin in Spain, thriving to embrace the spirit of the original sangha.
